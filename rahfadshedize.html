About Us

Welcome to Rahfad Restaurant and Bakery, where quality service and delicious food meet. We pride ourselves on serving fresh, high-quality meals with a touch of love. Whether you’re here for a quick bite or to enjoy a hearty meal, we’re committed to providing a delightful experience for every guest.

Our restaurant is open every day from 7 AM to 10 PM, ensuring that you can enjoy your favorite dishes at any time. Come in and taste the difference!
